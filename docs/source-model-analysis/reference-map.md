## Modeling Methodology

Policy clause structures are modeled on established enterprise-grade legal frameworks published by leading technology and platform companies. The purpose of this mapping is to:

1. Identify best-practice structural models for each policy area.
2. Ensure no clause gaps exist relative to recognized standards.
3. Enable reviewers to trace structural inspiration for each clause family.
4. Confirm no verbatim copying has occurred.

**Prohibition**: No source text is reproduced verbatim. Only clause structure, scope architecture, and limitation frameworks are referenced.

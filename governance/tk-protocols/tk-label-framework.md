## TK Label Classification (Placeholder)

| Label | Color Code | Access Level | Commercial Use | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| TK Open | Green | Public | Permitted | Community has designated this content as available for broad use |
| TK Restricted | Yellow | Community-restricted | Conditional | Access limited to defined communities or approved researchers |

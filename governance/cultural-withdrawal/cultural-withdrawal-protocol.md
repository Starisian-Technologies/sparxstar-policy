## Withdrawal Right Categories (Placeholder)

| Category | Description | Authority | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Sacred Content Withdrawal | Withdrawal of content designated as spiritually or ceremonially restricted | Elder or authorized community representative | Supersedes commercial licensing defaults |
| Seasonal Restriction | Temporary restriction of content during defined cultural periods | Authorized community representative | Time-bound; platform must enforce temporal restriction |
| Community Revocation | Community-initiated withdrawal of content submitted without proper consent | Community governance body | Platform must support repatriation workflow |
| Elder Override | Elder authority to override prior submission consent | Designated elder authority | Requires verification process |

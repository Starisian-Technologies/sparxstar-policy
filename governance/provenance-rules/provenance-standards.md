## Provenance Data Categories (Placeholder)

| Category | Description | Required | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Submitter identity | Identity of person submitting the artifact | Yes | Verified account required |
| Community of origin | Named community, culture, or tradition of origin | Where applicable | Optional for non-cultural content |
| Creation date / period | Approximate date or period of creation | Where known | |
| Rights holder | Named rights holder at time of submission | Yes | May differ from submitter |
| Prior publication | Whether content has been previously published | Yes | |
| TK designation | TK label assigned by authorized community | Where applicable | References TK Label Framework |
| Geographic origin | Region or country of origin | Where applicable | |
| Language of content | Primary language(s) of the content | Where applicable | |
| Chain of custody | Prior holders or custodians | Where applicable | Critical for cultural artifacts |

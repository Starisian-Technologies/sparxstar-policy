## Purpose

This index supports:
- Consistency review across policy documents
- Identification of conflicting provisions
- Tracking of clause-level changes across versions
- Drafting coordination between policy authors

## Usage Instructions

Each crosswalk file lists:
- The clause text or clause summary from each policy where the clause appears
- Any material differences between policies
- Required reconciliation actions

Crosswalk files are generated during the drafting review phase.

---

*Populate crosswalk files during policy drafting review phase. Do not create crosswalk files for policies that have not yet entered `drafting` status.*

## Scope

This policy governs **what content may exist on the platform and how it may be used**. It is distinct from the Community Policy, which governs human behavioral conduct.

Content Policy covers:

- Prohibited content categories
- Sacred and culturally restricted material
- Rights clearance requirements
- Submission legality and eligibility
- Derivative transformation permissions
- AI training eligibility
- Takedown eligibility and process
